# 3.4 Conclusion

This chapter has outlined the aims and objectives that will guide the development of the proposed cafeteria ordering system for United States International University – Africa (USIU-A). The general aim of the study was established as the creation of a mobile-based solution to address the operational inefficiencies of the current manual cafeteria process. This broad aim was then broken down into specific objectives that focus on surveying existing systems, designing and developing a functional prototype with real-time ordering, advance scheduling, and an in-app wallet, and finally testing and evaluating the system against the problems identified in Chapter One.

The formulation of these aims and objectives provides a structured roadmap for the project. They ensure that each stage of the design and development process remains aligned with the central problem while laying the groundwork for the practical decisions that follow. The objectives highlight the project's emphasis on user-centred design, technical robustness through Firebase's real-time and transactional capabilities, and operational relevance to the specific context of the USIU-A cafeteria. The following chapters translate these objectives into a concrete plan, system design, and implementation.
