# 4.5.1 Hardware Requirements

The USIU Cafeteria Ordering System will require the following hardware for development and testing.

## Development Machine

- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 (8th Gen or higher) or equivalent
- **RAM:** 8 GB minimum (16 GB recommended for running Android Emulator alongside Android Studio)
- **Storage:** 256 GB SSD or higher
- **Operating System:** Windows 10/11, macOS, or Linux

## End-User Devices

- **Android Smartphone:** Minimum Android 7.0 (API level 24) for students and staff. A physical device is preferred for testing GPS, FCM push notifications, and real-world performance.
- **Internet Access:** Required for Firestore real-time sync, Firebase Authentication, and Cloud Messaging. The application does not support offline mode.
