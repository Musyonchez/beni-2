# 4.2 Project Phases

The successful development of the USIU-A cafeteria ordering system depends on a carefully structured process that divides the project into manageable phases. Each phase represents a critical stage in the system's lifecycle, ensuring that the final product is both technically sound and user-centred. The phases follow a logical sequence beginning with research, moving through requirements gathering, design, implementation, and testing, before concluding with evaluation and documentation.
