# 2.1 Introduction

The purpose of a literature review is to examine existing knowledge, systems, and research that relate to the proposed study. By exploring global, regional, and local contexts, this chapter highlights how cafeteria and food ordering technologies have evolved, their adoption in educational institutions, and the challenges that remain unresolved. The review also identifies the specific gaps that justify the development of a dedicated mobile cafeteria ordering system for United States International University – Africa (USIU-A).
