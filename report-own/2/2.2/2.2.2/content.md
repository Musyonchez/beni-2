# 2.2.2 NUSmart Dining (National University of Singapore, Singapore)

The National University of Singapore (NUS) developed NUSmart Dining, a mobile application dedicated to campus food ordering. Through the app, students can browse canteen stalls, place orders, save favourite items for quick reordering, and track the status of current and past orders in real time. The order history screen records each transaction with its date, time, amount, and collection status, and flags any refunded transactions clearly. A Favourites tab allows students to curate a personal list of preferred dishes from across multiple stalls — each tagged with a rating, price, and stall category — enabling repeat orders to be placed in a few taps rather than navigating the full menu each time.

Figure 4 shows the Favourites screen, presenting the student's saved dishes with star ratings, prices, and stall categories across a scrollable list, enabling fast repeat ordering without browsing the full catalogue.

The NUSmart Dining app stands out for its combination of order tracking and personalisation. The order history with refund tracking provides students with a clear audit trail of their spending, while the Favourites feature directly reduces the friction of repeat ordering — a pattern common in cafeteria settings where students tend to reorder the same meals regularly. Both features are directly relevant to the problem this project addresses, where students currently have no record of past orders and must browse the full menu from scratch on every visit.

The system's limitations from a replication standpoint are similar to other institutionally integrated platforms: it depends on NUS's campus payment infrastructure and stall operator agreements. The app also does not appear to support advance scheduling of orders for a future meal slot. For the USIU Cafeteria Ordering System, the NUSmart Dining model informs the order history and wallet transaction tracking features, while the absence of pre-order scheduling in the NUS platform highlights an opportunity for this project to go further.
