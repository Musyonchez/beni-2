# 4.5.1 Hardware Requirements

The USIU Campus Navigation System will require both development and deployment hardware.

## Development Machines

- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 (8th Gen or higher) or equivalent
- **RAM:** 8 GB minimum
- **Storage:** 256 GB SSD or higher
- **Operating System:** Windows 10/11, macOS, or Linux

## End-User Devices

- **Smartphones (Android/iOS):** Users will primarily access the navigation system through mobile devices.
- **Computers/Laptops:** Access through a modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox).
- **Internet Access:** Required for accessing maps, generating routes, and retrieving updated campus information.
