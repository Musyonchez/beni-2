# 4.3.1 Phase One: Research, Requirements Gathering, And System Design

## i. Research

**Objective:** To understand navigation challenges at USIU and review existing global and regional campus navigation systems.

**Tasks:** Review global (MIT WhereIs, NUS NextBus) and regional (UCT, UoN) systems; conduct surveys and interviews with USIU students, staff, and visitors.

**Deliverables:** Research findings report; identified gaps in current solutions.

**Timeline:** Week 1

---

## ii. Requirements Gathering

**Objective:** To define what the system should do.

**Tasks:** Identify functional requirements (search, maps, route generation) and non-functional requirements (usability, scalability, accessibility).

**Deliverables:** Software Requirements Specification (SRS).

**Timeline:** Week 2

---

## iii. Proposal

**Objective:** To consolidate research and requirements into a structured plan.

**Tasks:** Draft abstract, background, literature review, aims & methodology tailored to USIU's needs.

**Deliverables:** Completed and approved proposal.

**Timeline:** Week 3

---

## iv. System Architecture and Design

**Objective:** To design the system structure and workflows.

**Tasks:** Develop use case diagrams, ERDs, DFDs, wireframes, and overall architecture blueprint for the navigation system.

**Deliverables:** System design blueprints and architecture document.

**Timeline:** Week 4
