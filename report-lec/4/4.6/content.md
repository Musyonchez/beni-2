# 4.6 Conclusion

This chapter has presented the methodology to be used in the design and development of the USIU Campus Navigation System, an interactive platform designed to simplify movement across campus. The work has been structured into three phases: Research and Design, System Development, and Testing & Reporting, each with well-defined objectives, tasks, and deliverables. The program of work and corresponding Gantt chart illustrate the timeline, dependencies, and milestones, ensuring that the project progresses in a systematic, efficient, and manageable way.
