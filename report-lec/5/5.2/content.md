# 5.2 Functional Requirements

Functional requirements define the specific actions and features that the Campus Navigation System must provide to meet the needs of students, staff, and visitors.

## Student Functional Requirements

- **FR1:** The system shall allow students to search for campus buildings, lecture halls, offices, and amenities (e.g., cafeteria, library, hostels).
- **FR2:** The system shall provide step-by-step navigation to guide students from their current location to a selected destination.
- **FR3:** The system shall allow students to filter destinations by category (e.g., academic, administration, social, or residential).
- **FR4:** The system shall allow students to view building details (room codes, department offices, operating hours).
- **FR5:** The system shall provide notifications or prompts when they are near their selected destination.

## Staff Functional Requirements

- **FR6:** The system shall allow staff members to update or verify campus building details (e.g., office location changes).
- **FR7:** The system shall allow staff to search and navigate to offices, classrooms, or student service areas.
- **FR8:** The system shall allow authorized staff to add or update administrative locations.

## Visitor Functional Requirements

- **FR9:** The system shall allow visitors to use a simplified mode to search for common destinations (e.g., admissions office, auditorium, parking, clinic).
- **FR10:** The system shall provide route guidance optimized for new users unfamiliar with campus layout.

## Administrator Functional Requirements

- **FR11:** The system shall allow administrators to manage building data, routes, and geolocation records in the database.
- **FR12:** The system shall allow administrators to approve or edit map changes before they go live.
- **FR13:** The system shall generate usage reports (e.g., most-searched buildings, peak navigation times).

## System-Wide Functional Requirements

- **FR14:** The system shall provide real-time GPS navigation with step-by-step directions.
- **FR15:** The system shall allow search and filter functionality for browsing destinations by name, code, or category.
- **FR16:** The system shall ensure secure authentication for students, staff, and administrators.
- **FR17:** The system shall maintain a centralized geospatial database for campus building and route data.
- **FR18:** The system shall provide a mobile-first design, ensuring accessibility on both Android and iOS.
